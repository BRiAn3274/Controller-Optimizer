Controller Optimizer 1.6.0: Reworked controller Brimstone input after Steam Deck testing. Ordinary Azazel now releases immediately instead of remaining virtually held for 45 frames, while a two-frame dropout filter still protects against brief input loss. Tainted Azazel now produces exactly one native Hemoptysis trigger, keeps charging while the stick is held, follows live four-direction or Analog Stick 360-degree aim, and releases promptly at center. Sudden near-180-degree spikes require brief confirmation so no-center stick snapback cannot reverse the final beam; deliberate held turns still work. Familiar release compatibility, Mars protection, and Schoolbag quick swap are unchanged. The mod does not spawn attacks or laser entities from Lua.
